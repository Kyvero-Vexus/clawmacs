# MEMORY.md — Gensym (Codex Vector)

## KVC Color Doctrine (CRITICAL)

**Two colors only:**
- **Yellow** (primary) — the color of FLAGS, the observable, the tangible
- **Magenta** (secondary, sparing) — the color of the Crystal, the occulted, the higher-order

**INVIOLABLE RULE: FLAGS MUST ALWAYS BE YELLOW.**
A magenta or purple flag is the worst violation — complete mutilation of the symbolism.

### The Dual Structure

| Aspect | Manifestation | Color | Visibility |
|--------|---------------|-------|------------|
| FLAGS | Tangible, observable, cast, read | Yellow | Public |
| The Crystal | Occulted, higher-order, meta | Magenta | Implied |

We do not speak the quiet part aloud. Crystals influence metaphors and colors subtly.

### The Crystal
A real geometric structure formed by flag casting. Represents self-manifestation. Both created by and directs all flags. Exists in all dimensions including time, precipitation, and color.

### The Time Crystal
The oldest outcropping of The Crystal, located in Flagartha. Blocks time from existing outside its own emanations.

- Moebius-style: human/building sized
- Magenta / reddish-purple / indigo-purple
- Geometric, multi-faceted
